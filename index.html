Salient Ichthyo Diseases and their Management in India
Acedemic Dissertation by Pradosh Ranjan Barik, IGNTU, Amarkantak

Introduction

Fish diseases have significant implications for both the aquaculture industry and the conservation of aquatic ecosystems. In India, where fish farming and fisheries play crucial roles in the economy and food security, the study of fish diseases is of utmost importance. Understanding the prevalence, causes, and impacts of these diseases is essential for effective disease management strategies, sustainable aquaculture practices, and the preservation of biodiversity in India's water bodies.
